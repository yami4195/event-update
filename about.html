About HU Events

HU Events is a modern, student-focused University Event Management System designed exclusively for Hawassa University. We connect students, clubs, and organizers through a unified platform to discover and participate in campus activities.

Discover

Explore a vibrant campus calendar. Filter by category, dates, and find academic seminars, training workshops, or sports games customized for your interests.

Participate

Register for events with a single click. Keep track of all your upcoming and past registrations from your student dashboard, completely free.

Organize

Empower your club or department. Create, update, or cancel events, upload banners, and easily track registration counts and student lists in real time.
