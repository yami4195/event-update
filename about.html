About HU Events

HU Events is a student-focused University Event Management System designed exclusively for Hawassa University.

Our goal is to make campus activities easy to discover and join. Students can browse events, filter by category, and register in a few clicks. Organizers can create and manage event details, track registrations, and keep activities active across the university community.

HU Events supports a variety of campus engagements, including:

  • Seminar and academic talks
  • Training sessions
  • Club programs
  • Sport events
  • Cultural events
  • Hackathons
